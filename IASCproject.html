Steve Nash

2x MVP, 7x All-NBA, 8x All-Star

14.3ppg, 3.0rpg, 8.5apg, FG 49%, 1217 games, 18 year career

I'm here to talk about my favourite NBA player of all time: Steve Nash!

Steve Nash will go down as one of the greatest players to ever grace the court. He was an excellent teammate, leader and competitor. To illustrate that point, at the bottom of this page lies a link to his statistics and accolades!
